Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef sirloin
  • 2-3 cups beef broth
  • 1⁄4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp garlic
  • 2 cups onion
  • 11⁄2 cups tomato
  • 3⁄4 cup red bell peppers
  • 3⁄4 cup green bell peppers
  • salt
  • pepper

Preparation

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Chop onion and tomato
  3. Slice bell peppers thin (julienne)
  4. Heat olive oil in a saucepan
  5. Sautee the garlic, onion, tomato, and peppers
  6. Add the beef, sautee for a few minutes
  7. Add the beef broth and tomato paste, simmer until beef is tender
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste
