Sinigang

Ingredients

  • Country style boneless pork ribs (or boston butt)
  • Sinigang mix
  • beef bouillon (cubes or powder)
  • mustard greens or kale or spinach
  • red radishes
  • 2-4 roma tomatoes
  • 1 bag frozen okra
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • water

Preparation

  1. Cut ribs into 1 inch chunks and place into large pot
  2. Fill pot with water generously. Try to have somewhere around 8 cups. The water you put in will turn into soup.
  3. Bring to boil on high heat. Once boiling, scoop out the fat that rises to the top so that the liquid is clear.
  4. Cook meat for 45 minutes and prepare vegetables in the meantime:
    • Slice onion in half and then slice each half into ~6 wedges
    • Wash and trim the radishes (rinse well since they often have sand/dirt in the leaves)
  5. After 45 minutes put in onions, radishes, sinigang mix and beef bouillon. Stir and taste, adding more buillon if necessary.
    • For adding the bouillon, estimate your water in cups and add half of that number in tablespoons
    • Just remember, it is better to start with too little than with too much!
  6. Once desired taste is reached then add your greens, followed by the okra and tomatoes. Stir (not too hard or the meat will shred!), then reduce heat to low-medium and cover the pot. Cook for 15 minutes, and then done!
